Overall Description

Product Perspective

      This inventory system application is the replacement for, not a current application, but a paper-based system which is used to manage the inventory in a medium-sized supermarket warehouse. This project began because Chen’s Supermarket, established in late 2018, requested an application to manage their inventory. They said that stock keeping was a major issue for them as files were easily lost or damaged. This system would allow Chen’s to easily track and manage their inventory.

      The users of this applciation would be:

  1. Warehouse Manager
  2. Product Clerk
  3. Warehouse Attendant

      Warehouse manager – The warehouse manager is the person tasked with ensuring the grocery’s warehouse department operates smoothly. They are responsible for the inventory, the purchase orders and the receiving of goods when the supplier delivers them. The warehouse manager will have the highest privilege in the system. They will be able to view supplier and employee information as well as search for purchase orders and inventory items. The manager can view a list of items which are associated with a specific supplier, create purchase orders and create new inventory.

      Product Clerk – The product clerk is responsible for the ordering of items from the suppliers, receiving items when they ae delivered and adding them to the warehouse’s stock. The product clerk would have privileges to create purchase orders, create new inventory (add received item to inventory) and create new delivery records (when the supplier delivers the ordered items). The clerks can edit the purchase order details, more accurately the status of the order. When a purchase order is created its status is set to “Open” this means that the order is yet to be delivered by the supplier. When the supplier makes the delivery, the clerk will create a new delivery record and then proceed to update the purchase order by changing the status to “Closed”. The product clerk can search for items in inventory, search for suppliers and search for items which are provided by the suppliers.

      Warehouse Attendant – The warehouse attendant has the least amount of privileges in the system. Their access in the system will be limited to only being able to search for inventory items in the system. They would not have access to any other portions of the system.
