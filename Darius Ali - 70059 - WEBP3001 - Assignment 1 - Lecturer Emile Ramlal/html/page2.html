Project Scope

      The scope of the project is to create a desktop application for to aid a supermarket warehouse in managing their inventory. The system in development needs to be able to produce monthly reports of the inventory of the supermarket warehouse. The staff currently use a paperbased system and it is a tedious process. The automation of the task would aid them in providing better performance in their daily duties.

      The warehouse has a varied number of staff and certain staff would have specific privileges within the system. The members of staff and their privileges are:

  • Manager – Search (for item, supplier, deliveries, purchase order), create (new inventory item, suppliers, deliveries, purchase orders), update (inventory item, supplier, delivery, purchase order), delete inventory item.
  • Product Clerk – Search (for item, purchase order, supplier, delivery), create (new inventory item, purchase order, delivery), update (inventory item, purchase order, delivery).
  • Warehouse attendant - Search (for item).

      The system must also be able to print reports of the vendors who supply the supermarket with their goods. These reports can also be done on a monthly basis to track the vendors for each month. Reports of deliveries should also be generated based on supplier and monthly reports of purchase orders must also be generated along with a damages report for all stock that was returned to the supplier.
